# GroundCUA: Grounding Computer Use Agents on Human Demonstrations

## TL;DR
> GroundCUA: improves CUA grounding by learning from human demonstration data that captures natural visual attention patterns and interaction styles; bridges the gap between VLM perception and human-like GUI interaction.

## Key Contributions
- Learns grounding patterns from human demonstration trajectories (gaze, click, interaction styles)
- Bridges VLM perception and human-like computer use
- Improves reliability of CUA actions on production-grade interfaces
