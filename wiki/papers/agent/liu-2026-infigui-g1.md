# InfiGUI-G1: Advancing GUI Grounding with Adaptive Exploration Policy Optimization

## TL;DR
> Adaptive Exploration Policy Optimization (AEPO) for GUI grounding; discovers that CoT reasoning decreases grounding precision; fast-thinking templates suppress reasoning for direct coordinate regression.

## Key Contributions
- AEPO: reward exploration in continuous coordinate spaces to prevent exploitation collapse
- Finding: CoT reasoning decreases grounding precision via hallucination
- Fast Thinking Templates: suppress reasoning, directly predict coordinates (System 1 mode)
- AAAI 2026 Oral; SOTA on ScreenSpot and ScreenSpot-Pro
