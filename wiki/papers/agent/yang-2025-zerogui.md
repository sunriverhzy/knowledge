# ZeroGUI: Automating Online GUI Learning at Zero Human Cost

## TL;DR
> ZeroGUI: zero-human-cost online GUI learning via aggregated VLM-as-judge scoring to construct self-evolving training curricula; no manual task or reward annotation.

## Key Contributions
- Zero human annotation: task generation and reward judgment fully automated
- Aggregated VLM-as-Judge: multiple model judges for reliable reward signal
- Self-evolving curriculum: harder tasks generated from agent's current capability frontier
