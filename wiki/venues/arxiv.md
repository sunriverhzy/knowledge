# arXiv

## Overview
**arXiv** is an open-access preprint server that has become the
*de facto* first publication venue for AI research. The vast majority
of landmark LLM, RL, and AIGC results are released on arXiv weeks
to months before any peer-reviewed venue. Most papers in this wiki
will originate here — even those later accepted at NeurIPS, ICML,
ICLR, etc.

We use `arxiv` as the venue slug for **preprints that have not yet
been peer-reviewed or whose peer-reviewed acceptance is not yet known**.
When a paper is later formally published, the venue tag is updated to
the accepted venue (e.g., `neurips`) and this page's listing is moved.
