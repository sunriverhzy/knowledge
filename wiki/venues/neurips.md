# Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS)

## Overview
**NeurIPS** (originally NIPS) is one of the three top-tier machine
learning conferences alongside ICML and ICLR. It is the premier venue
for theoretical and foundational work in ML, as well as for many
landmark applied results in deep learning, RL, and NLP. Acceptance is
highly competitive (~20–25% accept rate).

For this wiki, a paper is tagged with `venue: NeurIPS <year>` once it
is formally accepted, even if it continues to live on arXiv.
